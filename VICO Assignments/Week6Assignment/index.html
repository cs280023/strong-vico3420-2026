Instructions

Estimated Time: 15 minutes

I learned this recipe by lots of experimentation, plus I work on grill at Chipotle, so trust me I'm sorta credible!

  1. Get out your Defrosted Chicken Thighs, spread them out and push them down on a cutting board until they are flat.
  2. Grab a medium-sized bowl, A whisk, a pan, a pair of tongs, and a food brush.
  3. Add 1/4 cup of olive oil, 1 teaspoon of each normal seasoning, and 1/2 teaspoon of each spicy seasoning to the bowl.
    4. Note: If you know how to season well, you can adjust the amount of various seasoning based on your personal preference.
  4. Whisk your ingredients together until well combined.
  5. Use the food brush to coat your chicken thighs with the seasoning mixture. Make sure to cover all sides.
  6. Heat your pan to medium-high heat and some olive oil to the entire pan.
    7. Note: I like to use 1/4 of a paper towel and soak it in olive oil, then wipe the whole pan so it's throughly coated.
  7. Once the pan is heated, place your chicken thighs smooth side down, then lightly salt the top and flip after 5 minutes.
  8. After flipping, lightly salt again and cook for another 5 minutes.
    9. Note: When I've cooked it, this has ensured it's perfect every time, but if you want to be safe, use a thermometer to check the internal temperature of them all. Ensure it has reached 165°F (74°C), and it's safe for consumption.
  9. Take your chicken off the heat, and you're done! Enjoy!
